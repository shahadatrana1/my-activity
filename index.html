Sylhet tour is your perfect holiday destination during the monsoon and rainy season in Bangladesh. Sylhet is the holy mystical city & experience waterways. The best time to visit this thrilling place is during the rainy season. The water level rises higher than any other time and reveals its true beauty around this time of the year.
